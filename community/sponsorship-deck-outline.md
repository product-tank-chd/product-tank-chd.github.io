## Slide 5 — Sponsorship Tiers

### 🥇 Title Sponsor — ₹[XX,000] per event

Exclusive single sponsor. Your brand = the event.

- Logo prominently featured on all event materials (digital + print)
- Verbal recognition by MC at opening and closing
- 2-minute company introduction slot (not a sales pitch)
- Branded banner / backdrop at event (you provide)
- Logo on Meetup event page, email announcement, and all social posts
- Post-event social media thanks
- 5 guaranteed guest passes (for team members)
- Option to host at your offices

---

### 🥈 Drinks Sponsor — ₹[XX,000] per event

Fuel the networking.

- Logo on all social posts and email with "drinks provided by [Company]"
- Verbal recognition by MC
- Branded napkins / cups (optional — you provide)
- 2 guaranteed guest passes
- Post-event social media thanks

---

### 🥉 Community Partner — In-kind / ₹[X,000]

For companies contributing venue, prizes, swag, or partial sponsorship.

- Logo on Meetup event page
- Social media mention
- Verbal recognition on the night

---

### 🤝 Annual Supporter — ₹[X,XX,000] / year (3+ events)

Commit to multiple events and get enhanced benefits.

- All Title Sponsor benefits at each event
- Dedicated "community partner" mention in all between-event comms
- Priority event hosting (at your offices)
- Speaking opportunity (one event per year)
- Named in all channel bios / descriptions

---

## Slide 6 — What We Ask of Sponsors

- We are a **community event, not a sales platform**. Sponsored talks must be educational, not pitches.
- We reserve the right to decline content that doesn't align with our community values
- We require payment **before** the event
