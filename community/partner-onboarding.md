## Onboarding Checklist (confirmed sponsors)

- [ ] Sponsorship agreement / confirmation email sent and received
- [ ] Invoice raised (if applicable)
- [ ] Payment received
- [ ] Logo files received (SVG/PNG, both light and dark variants)
- [ ] Company description received (for Meetup page and social posts)
- [ ] Agreed deliverables documented (from sponsorship tier)
- [ ] Sponsor briefed on Code of Conduct and community guidelines
- [ ] Sponsor's team members added as confirmed attendees on Meetup

---

## Post-Event Deliverables to Sponsor

- [ ] Social media thank-you post published (share link with sponsor)
- [ ] Event photos sent (if sponsor requested)
- [ ] Attendance numbers shared
- [ ] Follow-up email sent to discuss next event
