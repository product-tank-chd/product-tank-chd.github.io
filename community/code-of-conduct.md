# Code of Conduct

ProductTank Chandigarh is dedicated to providing a harassment-free, inclusive community experience for everyone — regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, nationality, or company size.

We do not tolerate harassment of community members in any form.

---

## Expected Behaviour

All attendees, speakers, sponsors, and organisers are expected to:

- **Be welcoming and inclusive** — Make space for others, especially those newer to the community
- **Be respectful** — Differing viewpoints and experiences are valuable
- **Be constructive** — Critique ideas, not people
- **Be mindful of your words** — Avoid exclusionary language, jokes, or comments
- **Ask before photographing** — Always get consent before sharing photos of others

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

The following will not be tolerated:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Offensive verbal comments related to gender, sexual orientation, disability, ethnicity, religion, etc.
- Deliberate intimidation or following
- Unwanted physical contact or sexual attention
- Disrupting talks, presentations, or community discussions
- Sustained off-topic marketing, recruitment cold approaches, or pitching without consent

---

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please contact a ProductTank Chandigarh organiser immediately. Organisers can be identified by their name badges.

You may also report via email: **[organisers-email@example.com]**

All reports will be handled with discretion and taken seriously.

---

## Consequences

Anyone asked to stop unacceptable behaviour is expected to comply immediately. Organisers may take any action deemed appropriate, including removal from the event and banning from future events.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all ProductTank Chandigarh events, online community spaces (LinkedIn group, Slack, etc.), and any communications conducted in connection with our community.
