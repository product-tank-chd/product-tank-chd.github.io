## Between-Event Content Ideas

When there's no event to promote, keep the community warm with:

- Share a quote or insight from a previous talk
- "This week in product" — curate a link/article relevant to PMs
- Ask a question: "What's your biggest product challenge right now?"
- Highlight a community member or local product story
- Behind-the-scenes: organising a ProductTank event
- Repost content from Mind the Product / global ProductTank chapters
- Celebrate wins: "Congrats to [local company] on their launch!"
- Job shoutout: share PM/EM roles in Chandigarh tech companies
