## 2. Refined Meetup Description

ProductTank Chandigarh returns on Saturday, May 23, for an afternoon focused on what it really takes to build AI-native products once the demo ends and real product work begins.

This meetup is for teams thinking seriously about how intelligent products hold up in the real world: how they earn trust, stay reliable, scale well, and make commercial sense in fast-changing markets. We will explore product strategy, feedback loops, monetization, and lessons from teams building modern software products with both ambition and discipline.

Whether you are a Product Manager, founder, designer, engineering leader, AI product builder, or a developer curious about product thinking, you are welcome. Attendance is free, and venue and refreshments are being provided by Trantor Software Pvt. Ltd.

The session lineup will be announced soon. Our CFP is open, and we would love to hear from practitioners, founders, PMs, designers, and engineers who can share practical stories, frameworks, experiments, failures, and lessons learned with Chandigarh's growing product community.
