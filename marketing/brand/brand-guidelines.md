# ProductTank Chandigarh — Brand Guidelines

All event assets, social posts, email templates, and print materials should follow these guidelines to maintain a consistent, professional look that aligns with the global ProductTank / Mind the Product brand.

---

## Tone of Voice

- **Inclusive** — Everyone from fresh graduates to VPs is welcome
- **Conversational** — Friendly, not corporate; avoid jargon
- **Inspiring** — Celebrate learning and community growth
- **Concise** — Get to the point; respect people's time
- **Local pride** — Reference Chandigarh's growing tech ecosystem proudly

### Sample phrases to use

- "Join us for an evening of ideas, inspiration, and great conversations."
- "Whether you're a seasoned PM or just starting your product journey — you belong here."
- "Chandigarh's product community is growing. Be part of it."
- "Great talks. Great people. Great beer."

### Phrases to avoid

- "Synergise your learnings" (jargon)
- "Limited seats — book NOW!!!" (pushy)
- "This exclusive event" (exclusionary)
