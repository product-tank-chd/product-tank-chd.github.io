A community for product managers, engineering leaders, founders, and builders in Chandigarh and the Tricity region. We host conversations that help people learn from real product work, share practical experience, and build stronger product teams.

What we do

ProductTank is a global Mind the Product community format. Locally, we bring that spirit to Chandigarh through events, speaker sessions, peer learning, and open discussions about building better products.

For product people Talks, panels, and conversations for PMs and product teams.
For builders Practical discussion across design, engineering, research, and go-to-market.
For the community A welcoming space to connect, learn, and contribute.

About ProductTank

ProductTank is a community-led meetup brand from Mind the Product. It exists to help product professionals learn from one another, exchange ideas, and improve how products are discovered, designed, built, and grown.

ProductTank Chandigarh carries that idea forward with local events focused on practical insights, thoughtful conversation, and a healthy mix of product, engineering, and leadership perspectives.

Mission

To create a welcoming local space where product builders can share real experiences, learn together, and grow stronger product practices in the Chandigarh ecosystem.

  • Enable peer learning across product, design, engineering, and leadership
  • Highlight practical stories from local and visiting speakers
  • Build a community that is inclusive, useful, and consistent

Vision

To make ProductTank Chandigarh a trusted community hub for product thinking in North India, known for quality conversations, meaningful connections, and the kind of events people want to return to.

  • Support a stronger product culture in Chandigarh
  • Connect the local community with global ProductTank ideas
  • Help more people build products with clarity, empathy, and evidence
